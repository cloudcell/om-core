# OM Core Trademark Policy

`OM Core` is a trademark or potential trademark of Alexander Bikeyev.

Cloudcell Limited may use and administer the `OM Core` mark with the trademark
owner's permission.

This policy explains how the `OM Core` name and any official `OM Core` logos,
icons, slogans, and visual identity may be used by the community, contributors,
users, consultants, educators, and commercial partners.

This policy applies separately from the software license. The OM Core software
may be available under the GNU Affero General Public License version 3
(`AGPL-3.0`), but that license does not grant trademark rights.

## Purpose

The purpose of this policy is to:

1. Protect users from confusion about the source, quality, and authenticity of
   OM Core software and services.
2. Allow fair community use of the OM Core name.
3. Prevent misleading forks, rebranded distributions, or competing services from
   appearing to be official.
4. Preserve the reputation and identity of the OM Core project.

## Protected Mark

The protected mark is:

- `OM Core`

This policy also applies to any official OM Core logo, icon, product badge,
splash screen, visual identity, or confusingly similar mark used in connection
with modeling, planning, analytics, OLAP, calculation engines, multidimensional
modeling, or related software.

For clarity, this policy does not claim `OpenModeling`, `OpenM`, or `OM` as
protected project trademarks unless and until Alexander Bikeyev separately publishes a policy or
registration covering those marks.

## Permitted Uses

You may use the OM Core mark without prior written permission in the following
cases, provided that your use is truthful, non-misleading, and does not imply
endorsement by Alexander Bikeyev or Cloudcell Limited.

### Referring to the Project

You may refer to OM Core by name when discussing, reviewing, teaching, or
describing the project.

Examples:

- "This tutorial explains how to use OM Core."
- "Our internal finance model was built with OM Core."
- "This plugin integrates with OM Core."

### Community Discussion

You may use the OM Core name in blog posts, videos, documentation,
presentations, academic work, issue reports, and community discussion.

Examples:

- "OM Core installation guide"
- "Benchmarking OM Core against spreadsheets"
- "OM Core plugin development notes"

### Compatibility Statements

You may use the OM Core name to describe compatibility.

Examples:

- "Compatible with OM Core"
- "Plugin for OM Core"
- "Template for OM Core"

You must not imply that your plugin, template, integration, service, or product
is official unless Alexander Bikeyev or Cloudcell Limited has approved it in writing.

### Forks

You may state that your project is a fork of OM Core.

Example:

- "This project is a fork of OM Core."

However, the fork must use a different name and branding unless Cloudcell
Limited gives written permission.

## Restricted Uses

You may not use the OM Core mark in a way that suggests your product, service,
fork, company, website, package, or distribution is official, endorsed,
sponsored, or maintained by Alexander Bikeyev or Cloudcell Limited unless you have
written permission.

The following uses require prior written permission from Alexander Bikeyev or Cloudcell Limited.

### Rebranding or White-Labeling

You may not rebrand OM Core, or any confusingly similar version of the software,
as your own official product.

Examples of prohibited use:

- Selling a modified version as "OM Core Pro" without permission.
- Releasing a fork called "OM Core Cloud" without permission.
- Releasing a fork called "OM Core Enterprise" without permission.
- Replacing the logo and presenting the software as your own official OM Core
  platform.

### Hosted or Managed Services

You may not use the OM Core mark for a hosted, managed, SaaS, or multi-tenant
service unless you have written permission from Alexander Bikeyev or Cloudcell Limited.

Examples of prohibited use:

- "OM Core Cloud"
- "OM Core Hosted"
- "Managed OM Core Service"
- "OM Core-as-a-Service"

This restriction is separate from the software license. The AGPL-3.0 may grant
copyright permissions to run, modify, and distribute the software, including
network use obligations, but it does not grant permission to use Alexander Bikeyev's trademarks, product names, logos, or brand identity.

### Competing Products

You may not use the OM Core mark, or confusingly similar marks, for a competing
commercial modeling, planning, analytics, spreadsheet, OLAP, or calculation
platform.

Examples of prohibited use:

- "OM Core Enterprise" for an independently operated competing product.
- "OM Core Planning" for a competing commercial platform.
- A fork branded so similarly that users may believe it is the official OM Core
  project.

### Domain Names and Social Media Accounts

You may not register or use domain names, social media handles, app names,
package names, or organization names that imply official status.

Examples requiring permission:

- `omcore.cloud`
- `omcorepro.com`
- `omcore-platform.com`
- `github.com/omcore-enterprise`
- `@omcorecloud`

Community names are acceptable if they clearly avoid confusion.

Examples that may be acceptable:

- `omcore-notes`
- `awesome-omcore`
- `omcore-tutorials`
- `omcore-plugin-examples`

### Logos and Visual Identity

You may not use the OM Core logo, icon, splash screen, or visual identity as the
primary logo for your own project, product, company, fork, website, SaaS
platform, consulting service, or commercial offering.

You may use the official OM Core logo or icon in educational or descriptive
materials, provided that:

1. The use is accurate and non-misleading.
2. The logo or icon is not modified in a way that damages the brand.
3. The logo or icon is not used more prominently than your own name or branding.
4. The use does not imply endorsement or partnership.

## Naming Rules for Forks and Derivative Projects

If you publish a fork or derivative version of OM Core, you must use a distinct
name.

Acceptable examples:

- "CubeForge, a fork of OM Core"
- "ModelGrid, based on OM Core"
- "Acme Planning Engine, derived from OM Core"

Unacceptable examples without permission:

- "OM Core Plus"
- "OM Core Enterprise"
- "OM Core Cloud"
- "OM Core Pro"
- "OM Core for Business"

Forks and derivative projects must also remove or clearly distinguish official
OM Core logos, splash screens, application icons, and product identity unless otherwise permitted by Alexander Bikeyev or Cloudcell Limited.
