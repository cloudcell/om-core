## Instructions for New Contributors

1. Open a pull request with your changes.
2. Add a row to the table above with your details.
3. Include the exact sign-off text referencing the Contributor Grant of License
   below.
4. If your contribution is employment-related or contract-related, include an
   employer or client authorization statement.
5. Ensure your commit messages include a `Signed-off-by:` line.

Example commit sign-off:

```text
Signed-off-by: Your Name <your@email.com>
```

### Example Sign-off

```text
I agree to the Contributor Grant of License defined in CONTRIBUTOR-SIGNOFFS.md.
```

### Example Sign-off with Employer or Client Authorization

```text
I agree to the Contributor Grant of License defined in CONTRIBUTOR-SIGNOFFS.md.
My employer/client, [Company Name], authorizes this contribution.
```
